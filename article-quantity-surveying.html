What quantity surveying students need

Quantity surveying combines technical drawing, measurement, and estimation skills. Here's what you'll need for your studies.

Essential Tools

  • Scientific calculator (FX-991ES Plus)
  • Scale ruler (metric with multiple scales)
  • Set squares and T-square
  • Measuring tape (long and short)
  • Drawing board (A3)
  • Mechanical pencils and eraser
  • Graph paper and notebooks

Advanced Equipment

  • Compass set for drawing
  • Architect's scale ruler
  • Hard hat and safety vest (for site visits)

Pro Tip

A good scale ruler is your most important tool. Learn to read different scales accurately.

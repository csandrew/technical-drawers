Architecture Guide

Architecture checklist

Avoid overbuying. Pick a compact, reliable sketch kit that works for studio projects and model-making.

What you actually need for first year

Architecture beginners need the right drawing tools without the clutter. Start with a good sketchbook, set squares, mechanical pencils, and a reliable scale ruler.

Essential items

  • Sketchbook and tracing paper
  • 30cm/60cm set squares and T-square
  • Mechanical pencils, erasers, and a sharpener
  • Model-making knife and glue
  • Scale ruler (metric)
  • Compass set

Pro Tip

Choose tools that are durable and easy to replace. Your department may specify some items, so verify before you buy.
